ABOUT US

One Asia Investment Partners (”One Asia”) is a Singapore wealth management firm with offices in Singapore,Shanghai and Colombo.


Our core business is in growing the wealth of our investors and clients through our platform which carries
a variety of our in-house funds as well as through our external asset management services.

We believe in value adding to our clients who have given us their trust and maintaining this invaluable relationship remains our highest priority

To this end, we strive to provide quality, transparency, accountability and ultimately, success.

One Asia was built on the belief that long-term planning is required to create and maintain lasting personal wealth and that our success in the creation of wealth turns on our integrity and loyalty. Cherishing and valuing each and every one of our relationships is a part of our firm DNA and like our motto we aspire to nurture and grow with our clients and investors beyond capital.
